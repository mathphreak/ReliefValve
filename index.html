Relief Valve

Prevents dangerous Steam buildups by letting you juggle games between library locations.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Usage

ReliefValve now works properly, but only on Windows. I'd say it's now better than Steam Mover in some ways, but it will keep improving.

  1. Make sure Steam already knows about your existing libraries.
  2. Exit Steam (this is important).
  3. Download the latest release, extract it, and run relief-valve.exe.
  4. Wait for Relief Valve to populate all your Steam games.
  5. Check the box next to one or more games.
  6. Select a destination at the bottom of the window.
  7. Click "Move".
  8. Wait for your selected games to finish moving. A progress bar should appear below the footer you just clicked inside. It will animate and look cool.
  9. Relaunch Steam. Your games will be in their new locations.

Stack

ReliefValve is built on Electron and uses the Noto Sans fonts from Google, Yahoo's pure.css, Font Awesome, Zepto.js, and all this stuff.
