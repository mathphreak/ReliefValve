Migrating from Other Tools to Relief Valve

Instructions

  1. Configure your external Steam library following these instructions.

  2. Use Relief Valve to move all games to their proper locations.

    If you already had a Steam library and you were using your previous tool to move games there, this will be very fast.

    If not, this may take a little while. Your games will be moved properly and you will not have a duplicate of the game.
